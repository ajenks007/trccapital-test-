About TRC Capital Partners

A privately held Texas real estate firm with over 25 years of experience investing across multiple asset classes.

The Firm

Who We Are

TRC Capital Partners is a privately held firm that invests primarily in the Texas market. Over the past twenty-five years, TRC has invested both private and institutional capital in real estate assets with an aggregate value of over $950 million, including over $575 million in hospitality assets and over $400 million in commercial real estate.

Today, TRC is strategically focused on real estate acquisitions, development, and growth opportunities primarily in the Texas market. Notable TRC real estate projects have included BBVA Compass Plaza, Regions Financial Center, The Redstone Building, Houstonian Hotel Club and Spa, Hyatt Regency Town Lake, Hilton Dallas/Rockwall, and Hilton Westchase.

With over a collective 75 years of real estate experience and a team that has been working together for over a decade, TRC is one of the most seasoned and skillful firms in Texas.

How We Work

Our Investment Approach

01

Identify Value

From the outset, TRC demonstrated remarkable strengths in identifying underperforming and undervalued assets across Texas. Our deep market knowledge and local relationships provide a consistent proprietary pipeline of opportunities.

02

Recapitalize and Improve

TRC acquires assets with both equity and debt, then applies capital and expertise to reposition, renovate, and stabilize each property. We have a proven track record of transforming underperforming assets into institutional-quality investments.

03

Operate and Exit

Active, hands-on management is our differentiator. By operating our own assets, we control the tenant experience, manage costs, and optimize income, positioning each property for a superior exit at the right time.

Our Focus

Texas Is Our Market

TRC is deeply rooted in the Texas real estate market. Our focus on Texas is not incidental, it is strategic. We believe Texas offers some of the most compelling risk-adjusted real estate opportunities in the United States, driven by strong population growth, a diversified economy, and a business-friendly environment.

From the Houston CBD to the Texas Hill Country, TRC has invested across a diverse range of asset types including Class A office, boutique office, mixed-use retail, hospitality, and short-term residential. This diversification allows TRC to pursue the most attractive opportunities regardless of asset class.
