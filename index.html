Who We Are

A Privately Held Texas Real Estate Firm

TRC Capital Partners is a privately held firm that invests primarily in the Texas market. Founded in 1990 and formerly known as The Redstone Companies, TRC has invested both private and institutional capital in real estate assets with an aggregate value of over $1.4 billion, spanning commercial office, mixed-use retail, hospitality, and residential development.

TRC's key differentiator is not just the deals we do, it is how we operate. We are actively involved in the buildings we own, the tenants we serve, and the communities we are part of. That hands-on commitment to management is what sets TRC apart from firms that simply acquire assets and wait for an exit.

Our Strategy

Acquisitions. Development. Management.

TRC specializes in strong risk-adjusted return opportunities and adds value through active, hands-on asset and property management, not just ownership.

01

Acquire

We identify underperforming and undervalued real estate assets across Texas, acquiring them through a disciplined process built on 35 years of local market knowledge and institutional relationships.

02

Develop and Improve

From historic renovations like 1001 McKinney to ground-up hospitality development like the InterContinental Houston, TRC applies capital and expertise to create institutional-quality assets.

03

Operate and Grow

Active management is our differentiator. We control the tenant experience, manage operating costs, and optimize income, and building real relationships with the people who occupy our buildings.
