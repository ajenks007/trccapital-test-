Meet the Team

With a team that has worked together for over a decade, TRC brings a rare combination of institutional expertise and entrepreneurial execution to every investment.

Steve Lerner Chief Executive Officer

Steve Lerner serves as the CEO of TRC Capital Partners, LLC and TRC Ventures, LP (formerly The Redstone Companies, L.P.). Steve previously held the position of CEO of Redstone Companies Real Estate, LLC and Executive Vice President and General Counsel of The Redstone Companies, and served as an Executive Vice President of numerous Redstone-related entities. Prior to joining Redstone in 1998, Steve was a partner at the Houston law firm now known as Schlanger Silver LLP. Steve graduated with honors in Government from the University of Texas and is an honors graduate of the University of Texas School of Law in Austin, where he was a member of The Texas Law Review. Steve serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Harris County Improvement District #1 (the Uptown District) and on the Board of Directors of Veritex Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: VBTX) and Veritex Community Bank.

Kellie Jenks President

Kellie Jenks is the President of TRC Capital Partners, LLC and TRC Ventures, L.P. Kellie is responsible for strategy and execution of the investment and development platforms, as well as the newly formed property management division. Prior to joining Redstone, Kellie was a Vice President at Rockspring Capital and focused on developing institutional equity relationships. She also held positions as Investment Manager of a $400 million office portfolio at the Lionstone Group and an Associate at Goldman Sachs within the Whitehall Funds where she helped manage a $1.2 billion hotel investment platform. Kellie serves on the board of Buffalo Bayou Partnership and Hope Stone, Inc. She received her B.S. in Chemical Engineering from Texas A&M and her M.B.A. from Columbia Business School.
