Our View

Why TRC Invests Exclusively in Texas

  • 01

    Population and Job Growth

    Texas adds more people and jobs than virtually any other state. Houston alone is home to the world's largest medical complex, a leading energy industry, and a rapidly growing technology sector, all driving consistent demand for quality office and commercial space.

  • 02

    Business-Friendly Environment

    No state income tax, a streamlined regulatory environment, and a government actively competing for corporate relocations make Texas the destination of choice for businesses of all sizes. Every major relocation means more demand for the office assets TRC owns and operates.

  • 03

    Diversified Economy

    Texas's economy spans energy, healthcare, technology, finance, and manufacturing. That diversification insulates the market from single-sector shocks and creates steady, broad-based demand for commercial real estate across Houston and beyond.

  • 04

    Deep Local Knowledge

    TRC has operated exclusively in Texas since 1990. Our relationships, market knowledge, and deal flow are the direct result of 35 years of focused presence in this market. We do not try to be everywhere; we are committed to knowing Texas better than anyone.
